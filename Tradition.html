Christmas lights gives so much life to all the streets and churches. It is a picture perfect scene that we can also find in the cards. The twinkling lights are like stars that gives us hope.

White Christmas means some snowflakes fall and maybe more to make Frosty the snowman much fun. Snowball fights and sledding becomes part of tradition for this occasion. Then there's some hot cocoa awaiting with some cookies when we get back to the house.

Christmas wreath in all our doors to bring some good ole cheers. The decorations that makes us feel that life is so beautiful. The mistletoe hanging in the doorway that makes a couple blushed with excitement of a kiss. These simple ways of making this day full of wonder and good memories.
