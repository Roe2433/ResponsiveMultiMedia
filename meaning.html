Meaning

Christmas means giving - not just material things, but caring and loving. Just like the old song says "Give LOVE on Christmas day", it would be far more meaningful celebration when everyone feel love in their hearts. And will double it up when we are able to share that unconditional love.

Christmas means that unto us a child was born. A Savior for the world! The spiritual meaning of Christmas gives full reflection of what this glorious day is all about. It is about the incarnation of our Lord Jesus Christ to save all mankind from sins. Gloria to excelsis Deo!
