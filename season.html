Season
Meaning

Season of Saint Nicholas. Santa Claus comes on Christmas Eve with the reindeers in a sleigh, bringing gifts to all children all over the world. He goes down the chimney when everyone is asleep and put all the wrapped presents under the tree.

Season of Giftgiving. Children get gifts of toys that they wish on their list. The joy in their eyes when they open the boxes wrapped from under the tree, gives us the warmth feeling of what Christmas is about - giving joy.

Season of Baked Cookies. The smell of freshly baked cookies fill the air during Christmas Season. There are different kinds of cookies baked before Christmas. We used them as gifts to families and friends
